Wisk the flour, grated Naigamo (it becomes quite sticky!) and Dashi until you have a smooth texture. Put it in the fridge for 1 hour. Meanwhile cut the cabbage in small slices. Then mix the batter with the cabbage und the eggs.
Use a pan or your teppan grill and fry your batter for 5 minutes. Put the Bacon or the topping of your choice on top and flip the whole thing. Fry for 5 minutes then flip again. When it has a gloden brown colour, you can serve it on a plate, topped with the sauces, Aonori und Bonito.

How to make Okonomiyaki yourself

You can easily make Okonomiyaki at home. The recipes are available above and you don't need a high-end japanese restaurant kitchen. If you have a teppan grill, that's good, but a simple pan will also help and get you some nice round okonomiyaki. The more exotic ingredients, like Dashi or Nagaimo, are usually available in Asia supermarkets.
